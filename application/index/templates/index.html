Hello! I'm Jeanings and welcome to my tiny, tiny corner on the web that I use as a creative outlet and digital playground. What you see is technically my first-ever website, built from scratch using basic CSS, HTML, plain Javascript and served with Flask on Google's App Engine. Technicalities aside, a short background on how I got here.

In my early adult life, I did a bachelor's in materials engineering and then spent several years in a plastics plant's quality control lab. Looked like a proper chemist mixing concoctions and all that. Until I realized the only enjoyment I get from chemistry is extracting compounds of roasted beans of a certain tropical berry, and not from my actual occupation. So, I left that behind without much planned and took some time off to travel in Japan on a working holiday visa.

During my travels there, I had the opportunity to work in a B&B style inn right on a UNESCO World Heritage hiking trail rife with ginormous insects, all of which were very alien to this west coast Canadian. At that cozy inn where international guests come and go, I got to meet and chat with people from all walks of life. And it was from these conversations that led me to the idea of trying my hand at learning how to program by utilizing the countless free resources on the web with the small goal of building something — anything — that piques my interest.

So here we are. If you read all that and still have some time, take a peek at the projects section to see what keeps me busy.
