About

Hello (world)! First of all, thank you for visiting. I'm Jeanings and what you're seeing is technically my first-ever website, built from scratch using basic CSS, HTML, and vanilla Javascript in the front and served with Python and Flask in the back. Technicalities aside, a short background on how I got here.

In my previous life, I hold a bachelor's in materials engineering, spent several years in a plant's quality control lab before getting burnt-out and left that behind without much planned. From there, I took some time off to travel in Japan and tried new things that would've never crossed the mind of my previous risk-averse self.

During my travels, I had the opportunity to work in a b&b style inn right on a UNESCO World Heritage hiking trail rife with ginormous insects, all of which are unknown and may as well be alien life forms to this west coast Canadian. At that cozy inn where international guests come and go, I got to meet and chat with people from all walks of life. And it was from these conversations that led me to the idea of learning how to program by utilizing the countless free resources on the net with the small goal of building something - anything - that piques my attention.

So here we are. If you read all that and still have some time, take a peek at the gallery or head to the projects section to see what keeps me busy.
